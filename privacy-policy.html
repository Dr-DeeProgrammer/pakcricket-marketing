Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number when you create an account
  • Cricket Data: Match statistics, performance metrics, player profiles
  • Technical Information: IP address, device information, browser type
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, time spent

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and maintain the PakCricket platform
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Develop new features and services
  • Communicate with you about updates and security
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

Terms of Service

Acceptable Use

By using PakCricket, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information in your profile
  • Respect other users and maintain sportsmanship
  • Not engage in fraudulent activities or misrepresentation
  • Not attempt to hack or disrupt our services
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

User Responsibilities

As a PakCricket user, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your account
  • All activities that occur under your account
  • Ensuring your content complies with these terms
  • Reporting any security breaches or unauthorized access

Data Usage & Sharing

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Cricket Clubs & Scouts: Your player profile and statistics may be visible to registered scouts and clubs for talent identification purposes
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users

Your Rights & Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Object to certain data processing activities
  • Export your data in a portable format
