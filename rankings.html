The PakCricket Ranking System

Our algorithm is tailored to the Pakistani environment, weighing not just match results, but also verified individual performance and consistency.

How the Ranking Works

  1. Match Data Verification: Only matches recorded and validated on the app contribute to your score.
  2. Performance Metrics: Batting average, strike rate, wickets taken, and economy rate are weighted equally.
  3. Consistency Bonus: Players who participate and perform well across multiple leagues and grounds gain higher exposure.
