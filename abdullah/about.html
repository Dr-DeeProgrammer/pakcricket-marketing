About PakCricket

PakCricket is a modern platform created to discover and promote cricket talent across Pakistan. We aim to simplify cricket networking for players, coaches, and fans through one easy-to-use app.

Our Mission

We believe every talented player deserves a chance to shine. Our mission is to make cricket opportunities accessible for everyone, no matter where they come from.

The Problem

Many young and skilled cricketers go unnoticed because of poor access to talent platforms. Existing apps are often complex, slow, or designed for professionals only.

The Solution

PakCricket solves this by offering an easy, all-in-one mobile app for player profiles, match updates, and talent discovery — designed specifically for local cricket communities.

Our Vision

In the next five years, we aim to become Pakistan’s top digital cricket community — connecting every player, coach, and team in one place.

Founding Story

PakCricket started as a Final Year Project (FYP) by passionate students who wanted to merge technology and sports. What began as a small idea has now become a national-level digital initiative.

Our Core Values

  • Accessibility: We make cricket opportunities open to everyone.
  • Talent: We focus on recognizing and rewarding real skill.
  • Community: We build a platform where players support each other.
